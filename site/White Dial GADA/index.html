    White Dial GADA

    Idea

    A stainless steel, sports watch with mechanical movement that can be worn with almost every outfit/occasion, be maintained for a lifetime, and has as many "quality of life features” as possible

    Dream Specs

    • White dial
    • 6-o'clock date window
    • Stainless steel bracelet
    • Scratch resistant
    • ==Brushed, Explorer-style case?==
    • Highly serviceable/source-able parts
    • Thin & light
    • ==Drilled-lugs?==
    • 4Hz+
    • In-case regulation
    • "True/Flyer" GMT movement with 3-hands for quick hour setting
    • No plastic
    • Micro-adjust bracelet
