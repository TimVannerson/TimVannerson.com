# Digital Garden

## What is a Digital Garden?
A digital garden is a place where you can cultivate or observe ideas, knowledge, and projects in a non-linear and organic way.

Other information systems, like blogs or most note-taking apps, utilize chronological or hierarchical structures. A digital garden is cultivated through the linking of files, allowing one to "stroll through the garden", reviewing ideas & knowledge, making new connections, and adding or pruning "plants" as you go.
