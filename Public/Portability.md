# Portability
Portability is a desirable property of all systems because it decreases switching costs and enables maximizing output.

Because we tend to underestimate switching costs, any systems that minimize them helps maintain velocity when unforeseen disruptions occur in life.

Information and tools that are portable help us maximize output by allowing it to occur in more places, more frequently.

Portable solutions also tend to be more [simple](Simplification.md).
