# Zero to One
Going from zero to one is the hardest increment in any endeavor. 

You can always make incremental improvements on *something*, but you cannot compound 0 into anything greater.

Therefore, immense value is created in reducing the friction from 0 to 1. But be careful of [Mt. Stupid](Dunning-Kruger%20Effect.md) because true expertise is only built through the long-tail of [compounding](Compounding.md).
