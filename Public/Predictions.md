# Predictions
When making predictions, don't try to specifically guess *the* singular outcome. Rather, consider multiple, plausible outcomes and if possible, assign relative likelihoods to each. 

Most importantly, consider how you would prepare and/or respond in each scenario. Knowing the future(s) is only useful if it helps you make better decisions now.

"One who claims to know the future is a fool. One who claims to know many future may be wise. But one who only knows the present is wisest."
