# Coffee Brewing Methods
### Immersion
Coffee brewed with the grounds immersed in water; contrasted with percolation brewing.
### Percolation
Coffee brewed with hot water flowing through the grounds; contrasted with immersion brewing.
### Espresso
Coffee brewed with hot water **under pressure** through fine ground beans. Characterized by small serving volumes, quick brew times, and a foam called *crema*. A subtype of percolation brewing.
### Cold Brew
Coffee extracted in room-temperature or colder water. Due to low temperatures, *much* longer extractions times are necessary. A subtype of immersion brewing.
