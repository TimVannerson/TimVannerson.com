# Warm German Potato Salad
#### Ingredients
- [ ] Red potatoes
- [ ] Bacon
- [ ] Dijon mustard
- [ ] Apple cider vinegar
- [ ] Scallions
- [ ] Salt & pepper
#### Preparation
1. Measure all ingredients with your heart
2. Chop/cube red potatoes. Chop/slice scallions
3. Parboil in pot of salted water
4. Fry bacon in large cast iron or sauté pan; leave rendered fat in pan or reserve between batches
5. Lower temp to medium-low and re-add any reserved bacon fat
6. Mix in dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, sal, & pepper to desired consistency/taste
7. Drain potatoes, drizzle mustard sauce, toss to coat, and garnish with scallions
