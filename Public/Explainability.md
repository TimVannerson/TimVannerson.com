# Explainability
Hypotheses or results should be supported by plausible or confirmed mechanisms.

Building systems with explainability also enables quicker assessment of discrepancies between hypotheses and results.

The ability to explain complex systems is a demonstration of mastery to strive for and to seek out in others.

At best, opaque systems expose us to risk. At worst, accepting unsubstantiated claims without scrutiny fuels extremism.
