# Outrage is Engaging
Triggering outrage is one of the easiest ways to drive engagement from people.

Use this knowledge sparingly, as outrage has a low signal-to-noise ratio, is draining to the target, and they can respond unpredictably once engaged by it.

The knowledge is best used by simply heightneing our awareness to it so as to avoid being dragged into unproductive gossip, angst, or propaganda.
