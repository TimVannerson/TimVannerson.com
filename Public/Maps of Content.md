# Maps of Content
A map of Content is a note (or part of a note) that links to many related notes. It can contain unique information of it's own or simple be a collection of links.

Similar to a table of contents, although not necessarily chronological and often dynamic, maps of content act as a hub to related information.

Within a digital garden that does not utilize directory or timeline structures, maps of content help organize information.

An example of a map of content within [my own digital garden](My%20Digital%20Garden.md) is the [Frameworks](Frameworks.md) page.
