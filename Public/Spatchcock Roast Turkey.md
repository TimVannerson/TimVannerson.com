# Spatchcock Roast Turkey
#### Ingredients
- [ ] Whole turkey; 1-1.5 lb. per person (giblets not required but can be used to make stock/gravy)
- [ ] Compound butter (per 6lbs. of turkey)
- 1 stick unsalted butter
- 1 Tbsp. (~3g) rosemary; chopped 
- 1 Tbsp. (~3g) sage; chopped 
- 1 Tbsp. (~3g) thyme; chopped 
- 3 cloves garlic; minced/grated
- [ ] Dry rub (per 4lbs. of turkey)
- 1 Tbsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp smoke paprika
- Optional: 1/2 tsp dry, poultry herbs (above)
#### Preparation
1. T-minus 4+ days
	1. Thaw turkey (6 hour/lb. in fridge, 30 min./lb. in cold water)
2. T-minus 3 days: 
	1. Spatchcock turkey; save neck, giblets, & spine for stock  
	3. Season 1 Tbsp kosher salt + 1/2 tsp ea.  garlic powder, paprika, & dry herbs (“Deliciously Dill”), / 4 lbs turkey  
	4. Dry brine in fridge for ~48 hours
3. T-minus 1 day:
	1. Soften butter, chop fresh herbs, and mix into compound butter
	2. Spread compound butter on all parts of turkey under the skin
	3. Store in fridge overnight
4. Meal day:
	1. Preheat oven to 450F & insert leave-in thermometer to thickest part of turkey breast. Turkey will take approximately 6 minutes per pound
	2. Roast at 450F for 30 minutes and then lower oven to 425F
	3. Continue cooking until internal temp of breast reaches 165F
	4. Let rest for 15 minutes before carving
